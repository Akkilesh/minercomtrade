Our Work in Supply Chain

A Portfolio of Measurable Outcomes. Delivering Freshness Across Borders

The Challenge

Exporting onions over a 40–45 day sea voyage from JNPT (Mumbai) to Seychelles is a high-risk task.

  • Onions are perishable, prone to spoilage during long transit.
  • Buyer expectations were clear: fresh, quality onions on arrival.
  • Any delay or mismanagement could lead to loss of cargo and buyer trust.

Our Solution

At Minercom, we offered an end-to-end solution designed for success:

  • Careful Crop Selection – Only superior-grade 55+ size onions sourced for long-haul durability.
  • Optimized Packaging – Ventilated mesh bags for airflow and shelf-life extension.
  • Cold Chain Logistics – Reefer containers set at precise temperatures to preserve freshness.
  • Execution Excellence – Streamlined coordination with logistics partners to ensure timely handling and monitoring.

Execution

Minercom’s team managed the entire operation from sourcing to shipment. Every stage — procurement, packaging, loading, and container monitoring — was executed with precision and accountability.

Results

successful Delivery

At Minercom Trade and Export LLP, we don’t just ship products — we deliver solutions.

  • Three successful shipments delivered to Seychelles.
  • Zero spoilage, 100% freshness maintained.
  • Buyer confidence gained, resulting in 6 additional confirmed shipments.

Impact

This case demonstrates how Minercom, even as a growing exporter, is capable of:

  • Tackling complex export challenges to new markets.
  • Designing solutions that work for perishable commodities.
  • Building trust and repeat business in international markets.
